My first proper game and a high school graduation project. Spaceships is a real-time strategy game where players control a fleet of combat spacecraft in a battle against an AI opponent. Navigate through an asteroid-filled battlefield, manage your fleet composition, and destroy the enemy mothership to claim victory. Command your fleet. Outsmart the AI. Conquer the battlefield.

Spaceships is a fast-paced real-time strategy (RTS) game where you take command of a fleet of strike crafts and battle against an adaptive AI opponent. Navigate through a hazardous asteroid field, strategize your fleet composition, and engage in dynamic space combat to destroy the enemy mothership.

Features

  • ⚔️ Tactical RTS Combat – Lead your fleet in intense space battles.
  • 🧠 Adaptive AI Opponent – Face an AI that reacts to your tactics.
  • 🛠️ Customizable Fleet – Configure your spaceship roster to adjust difficulty.
  • 🎮 Modifiable Stats – Easily tweak spaceship attributes for a personalized experience.
  • ⏳ Time Control – Speed up or slow down the action for better strategic control.

Gameplay Overview

As the commander of a fleet, your ships launch from a powerful mothership carrier. Your mission is to destroy the enemy mothership while maneuvering through a randomly generated asteroid field. Each spacecraft has unique strengths and weaknesses, making fleet composition and tactical positioning crucial. Choose your engagements wisely—outsmarting your opponent is key to victory!

  • Overall basics of game development.
